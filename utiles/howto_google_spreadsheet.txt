Edit > Paste values only.
----------------------------------------------------

You can copy the cell values and when pasting:
Right-click
Paste Special...
Paste values only

------

[ Copy values ]
1. In cell C1, press Ctrl+C to copy (shortcut key for Windows).
2. In Cell D1, press Ctrl+Shift+V or right click on cell D1, select Paste special and apply paste values only.

[ Copy Formula ]
1. Ctrl+C to copy.
2. Alt+E+S+F to paste formulas only.

------
----------------------------------------------------
Enter the value in one of the cells.
----------------------------------------------------
Select your range with your cursor by grabbing from the first cell and dragging (no need to hold the blue cross in the corner, but the range should be highlighted when you are finished)
Type CTRL + Enter (linux)
