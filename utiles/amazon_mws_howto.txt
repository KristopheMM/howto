------------------------------------------------------------------------------
*** MWS Flow
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.0 Make Order ( first 30min customer can Cancel the Order)
	1.1	Pending (until 30min) - Amazon processing time ( No changes possible )
	1.2 An order in pending status cannot be canceled or confirmed.

2.0 Unshipped - ( can be Canceled with OrderAcknowledgement "Failure" StatusCode )
	2.1 Customer becomes notificatioin by Email if there is no payment issue

3.0 Order Fulfillment and Feedback
	3.1 Once the payment is authenticated, you will receive a “Sold, Ship Now” notification email from Amazon.
	3.2 After confirming the order, you can proceed to fulfill it.
	3.3 Now you have to confirm the shipment.

** The order must be confirmed within 30 days of the “Deliver” date given in the shipping column or the order will get canceled.
** Amazon will send you an email notification if the shipment is not confirmed on time.
